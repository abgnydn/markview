**Q: Can I use `@markview/core` for free in an open-source project?**
A: Yes! If your project is also open-source under an AGPL-compatible license, you can use it freely.

**Q: Can I evaluate `@markview/core` before purchasing?**

**Q: What if I'm building an internal tool?**
