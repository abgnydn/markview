The merge: finish the dolly.

The existing landing already promised a 3D vault. The CTA was breaking that promise by cutting to a flat editor. Instead of two products glued together, one continuous experience:

  • / shows the cosmic landing (existing). CTA "Open your vault" dollies the camera into the scene.
  • The same 3D context becomes /vault — planets become note-nodes, wikilinks become edges.
  • Click any node → camera focuses → editor expands as a panel in 3D space. Esc returns to the graph.
  • The Face (`/temple`, gated) is the same world a level deeper — the visitor speaks to it; it remembers them.
  • The Bridge (`@markview/mcp`) is what lets every other AI tool reach in without breaking the local-first promise.
